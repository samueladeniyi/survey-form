New Employee Survey Form

Instructions to Manager regarding New Hire Survey:



Please give the employee the following questionnaire after their first three months with your area.
Then meet with the employee to discuss their answers and potential areas of improvement for
management/the department moving forward, both for this employee and for future hires.

Three-Month Survey







1. My supervisor has clearly communicated my job responsibilities to me.

2. I have the tools and resources necessary to do my job well.


3. At work, I am consistently able to put my talents to work in my position.


4. My supervisor recognizes the good work that I do on a regular basis.
